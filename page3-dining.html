DINING

OGA'S CANTINA

Step up to the cantina at Black Spire Outpost and you’ll be met with the aromas of exotic drinks and the sound of animated patrons’ lively chatter (or is it fighting?) inside. If you’re looking for action and intrigue in the Outpost, you’ve come to the right place. Here you’re just as likely to find a job as you are a good drink. The cantina is where business gets done on Batuu.

Oga Garra

The proprietor of Oga’s Cantina takes great pride in her establishment and is always looking for new ways to bring in customers.

DJ R-3X

Former pilot of the Star Tours Starspeeder 3000, RX-24, found himself on Batuu through unknown circumstances. Thanks to the local droid expert, Mubo, RX-24’s programming was altered into the DJ now known as DJ R-3X at Oga’s Cantina.

Ronto Wraps

Head over to Ronto Roasters to try the infamous Ronto Wrap, featuring two cuts of tender ronto meat, slaw and the owner, Bakkar’s, signature Clutch Sauce. It’s smoky, spicy, and makes this sandwich disappear faster than a podracer.

Blue and Green Milk

At Wamba Family Farm’s Milk Stand you can try the fresh blue milk from the Wamba’s banthas raised just outside Black Spire, or try the rarer green milk harvested from thala-sirens.
